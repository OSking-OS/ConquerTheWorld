How to Play

Scroll / drag to navigate the map. +/− to zoom.

Click your country to select it, then choose an action:

⚔️ Attack: Click Attack Mode, then click an adjacent enemy country. Need 2+ troops.

🛡 Fortify: Move troops from one of your countries to a neighbor.

➕ Recruit: Spend gold to train troops (10 gold = 1 troop).

💰 Income: Each territory gives gold per turn. Larger countries give more.

End Turn — AI enemies take actions, then you collect gold income.
